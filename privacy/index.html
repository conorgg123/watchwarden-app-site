WatchWarden Movie Night

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

WatchWarden helps you plan what to watch, manage watchlists, run local pass-the-phone group sessions, and use movie-night decision tools. The app does not use advertising tracking or sell personal data.

Data Stored by the App

WatchWarden stores personal watchlist data locally on your device by default. This can include titles, watch status, ratings, notes, preferences, achievements, and app settings.

iCloud and CloudKit Sharing

Partner sharing is currently unavailable while it is upgraded to named, private CloudKit participants. The release build does not create or redeem public partner invitations.

Pass the Phone group sessions stay on the device. They are not advertised or operated as cross-device sessions.

Accounts and Sign-In

WatchWarden supports Sign in with Apple. Authentication identifies the account and isolates its personal records and preferences from other accounts on the device. WatchWarden does not fabricate an email address when Apple does not provide one.

Movie and TV Metadata

WatchWarden may request movie and TV metadata from TMDB when a live API key is configured. These requests are used for app functionality such as search, discovery, posters, and content details.

Purchases

The release-default app has no subscription paywall and does not collect purchase history.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, WatchWarden may use local or remote notifications for reminders and shared activity updates. You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Tracking and Advertising

WatchWarden does not use third-party advertising tracking and does not sell personal data.

Privacy Choices

You can manage app data and notification choices in the app and iOS Settings. Account deletion first removes or revokes remote sharing, then removes account-owned local records and preferences; incomplete deletion remains pending for retry. Additional details are available on the Privacy Choices page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@watchwarden.app.
