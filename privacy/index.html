WatchWarden Movie Night

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Overview

WatchWarden helps you plan what to watch, manage watchlists, invite a partner, run group watch sessions, and use movie-night decision tools. The app is designed to avoid advertising tracking and does not sell personal data.

Data Stored by the App

WatchWarden stores personal watchlist data locally on your device by default. This can include titles, watch status, ratings, notes, preferences, achievements, and app settings.

iCloud and CloudKit Sharing

When you use partner sharing, WatchWarden uses Apple iCloud and CloudKit Sharing to create invite links and synchronize shared content with people you choose. Shared records may include partner links and shared watchlist activity needed for the feature to work.

Group watch sessions use CloudKit records so participants can join by code and see shared session state. Group session records can include the session code, host/member display names, votes, wheel items, vetoes, status, and timestamps needed for the group feature.

Accounts and Sign-In

WatchWarden supports Sign in with Apple. Google sign-in is hidden unless a production Google OAuth client is configured. Authentication is used to identify the app session and support account-backed features.

Movie and TV Metadata

WatchWarden may request movie and TV metadata from TMDB when a live API key is configured. These requests are used for app functionality such as search, discovery, posters, and content details.

Purchases

If subscriptions are enabled, purchases and restoration are handled by Apple through StoreKit. WatchWarden reads StoreKit product and entitlement state to unlock paid features.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, WatchWarden may use local or remote notifications for reminders and shared activity updates. You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Tracking and Advertising

WatchWarden does not use third-party advertising tracking and does not sell personal data.

Privacy Choices

You can manage app data and sharing choices in the app, iCloud, and Apple account settings. Additional details are available on the Privacy Choices page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@watchwarden.app.
