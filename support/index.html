Common Topics

  • Partner sharing uses iCloud and CloudKit Sharing. Make sure both people are signed into iCloud.
  • Google sign-in is hidden unless a production Google OAuth client is configured.
  • Subscriptions and purchase restoration are handled by Apple through StoreKit.
  • Movie and TV metadata may use TMDB when a live API key is configured; otherwise the app can fall back to its bundled catalog.
