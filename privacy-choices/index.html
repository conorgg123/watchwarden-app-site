Privacy Choices

Last updated: May 9, 2026

In-App Controls

You can manage your personal watchlist, preferences, notifications, partner connection, and account state from inside WatchWarden. Personal watchlist data is stored locally by default.

iCloud Sharing

Partner sharing is unavailable in this release while it is upgraded to named, private iCloud participants. Pass the Phone group sessions stay on the device and do not use CloudKit.

Purchases

This release does not collect purchase history and does not provide a subscription paywall.

Access or Deletion Requests

For help accessing, exporting, or deleting app-related data that is not already controlled on your device or in iCloud, contact support@watchwarden.app.
